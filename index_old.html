Beginners

Beginners learn to enjoy dance while holding their heads up and bodies upright. We make the class interesting by introducing scarves and enjoyable floor exercises. We teach children to use facial expressions so they understand how to act a story. They also learn to move to music with simple steps, pointing feet, skips, jumps, runs making use of the space.

Intermediate

Pupils are now wanting to be challenged after having fun as beginners. They learn to keep in time with the music. We teach them to show expression and feeling, especially with the arms. In the early stages co ordination is a slow process. Those with a love of dance enjoy the challenge of this performing art. Students will learn technique at the barre and flexibility exercises. Pupils develop their personalities and gain confidence in themselves through their love to learn dances and performing. Which helps . This training will cover up to grade five.

Seniors

Senior students learn how to truly preform on stage at this level. They use their whole body and facial expressions to reflect the music and express themselves. They also develop their technique to enable them to master the advanced grades. The time spent in the senior levels lay the foundation for a career in dance but mastering ballet also gives lifelong enhancements to health, flexibility, and coordination.

Classes
