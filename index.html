Beginners

With beginers around the age of 5-7, we teach basic steps and basic coordination. We start out with facial expressions and creating a story, making it more fun. It isn't strict and is supposed to just give a nice start into learning ballet.

Intermediate

With intermediate around the age of 8-12, we teach more complex steps that requires more coordingation. We start giving longer dances to learn and teach the students to have more musicality. We teach different styles of ballet, from russian and greek dances to more classical ballet.

Seniors

With seniors around the age of 13-19, we expand their repertoire of steps even more, and high levels of coordination is achieved. In the senior level, we refine their technique as well as teaching them how to get a flow of a dance. We give multiple dances to practice for the end of year preformance.

Classes

We welcome Beginners and Intermediate age students to sign up. The first class is free as a trial to see how much the student enjoys it.
